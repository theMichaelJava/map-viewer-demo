The American War for Independence

A Hand-drawn Map Celebrating the Founding Story of The United States of America

$29
Add to Cart

Every nation has a founding mythology, but that does not mean the events didn't happen. For Americans, our founding story is rooted in Paul Revere's midnight ride, the shot heard around the world at Lexington, and the bold crossing of the Delaware by our national hero, George Washington.

It is anchored in the Declaration of Independence, the formal and defiant claim that these colonies would stand as a free and independent nation. This hand-drawn map highlights major battles on both land and sea, along with a visual timeline of key events that shaped us as a people and forged a nation.

Hanging this map on your wall is more than decoration. It is a celebration of more than 250 years of American history and the enduring ideals that shaped a nation.
